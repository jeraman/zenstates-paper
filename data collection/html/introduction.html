Welcome!

Thank you for your interest in participating in this study! It is based on three basic moments:

  • In the first moment, you will be asked to fill out a profiling questionnaire about your background;
  • In the second moment, you be asked to interpret interactive spaces described in three different models, predicting possible outcomes;
  • In the third moment, you will be asked fill out another short questionnaire based on the experience you just had.
The experiment will require about one hour to complete and there are no anticipated harms or risks. Your participation is entirely voluntary. You may discontinue participation in this study at any time by contacting the experimenter.
